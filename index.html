Basic info

Rabbits are little creatures with long ears and puffy tails, and they move their nose up and down in an adorable way. They eat the most orange vegetables in our world, and they reproduce more than any human ever has.

How to eat rabbits?

It's very easy! Just put them in the special machine or use a knife or other sharp tool (because if you kill it, rabbit wouldn't be able to escape) After that take rabbit's meat. You can eat it raw or cook it.
